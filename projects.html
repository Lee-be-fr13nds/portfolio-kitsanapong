Lumine Life – 2D Platformer & Minimalist Narrative
My Role:
  • Game Designer (Core Mechanics & Pacing)
  • Narrative Writer (Minimalist Narrative Design)
  • Level Designer (Blueprints Level Design)
Short Description:

Lumine Life is a philosophical 2D platformer that delves into the fractured consciousness of the player. Guided only by The Lantern of Light, the player must collect Three Runes of Memory (Love, Success, and Vibrancy). Each Rune reveals a forgotten truth through demanding physical challenges, momentous choice: To return to life and face reality, or to embrace eternal rest in peaceful oblivion.

Systems I Developed:
  • Established Narrative-Gameplay Fusion
  • Authored and Paced Full Game Script
  • Designed Level Blueprints
  • Defined Core Philosophical Conflict
Challenges & Solutions:
  • Wrote the complete final script, prioritizing conciseness and Text-Only
  • Established the game's climax as a Philosophical Choice
Tools & Tech Used:

Unity | C#
